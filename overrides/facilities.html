Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI)

Our MRI facility provides high-resolution structural and functional imaging. Capabilities include fMRI, diffusion weighted imaging, and spectroscopy, supporting a wide range of cognitive and clinical neuroscience research.

Learn more

Magnetoencephalography (MEG)

MEG records the magnetic fields generated by neural activity with millisecond temporal resolution and excellent spatial localisation — ideal for studying the timing and dynamics of cognitive processes.

Learn more

Electroencephalography (EEG)

EEG measures electrical brain activity via scalp electrodes with exceptional temporal resolution. Portable, cost-effective, and compatible with TMS and MRI for powerful multimodal experimental designs.

Learn more

Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS)

TMS uses magnetic fields to non-invasively stimulate targeted brain regions, allowing researchers to establish causal links between brain activity and behaviour. Can be combined with EEG for simultaneous recording.
