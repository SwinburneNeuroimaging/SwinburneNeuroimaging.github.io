# How do I come use these facilities?

## Contact us!
We can help you with all steps of your research such as, project conception, project design and logistical planning, advice on research ethics, advice on data management and providing expert training in data analysis. 

Head on over to our [Start_a_project](../start_a_project/index.md) page and it will give you more information.

Contact the team to discuss your project via [neuroimaging@swinburne.edu.au](mailto:neuroimaging@swinburne.edu.au).

Once contact has been made, you can begin an application.
